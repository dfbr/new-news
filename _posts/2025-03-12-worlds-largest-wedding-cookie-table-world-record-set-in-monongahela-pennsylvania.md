title: World's Largest Wedding Cookie Table, world record set in Monongahela, Pennsylvania
---

Monongahela, Pennsylvania, United States--The wedding cookie table set up by the Monongahela Area Historical Society as part of its celebration of the 250th anniversary of Monongahela's founding held 88,425 cookies, which were served at three weddings and three vow renewal ceremonies, setting the new world record for the World's Largest Wedding Cookie Table, according to the WORLD RECORD ACADEMY.
